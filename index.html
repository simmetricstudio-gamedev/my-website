Simulation

I am a huge fan of real-time strategy and simulation games. I grew up on the Rollercoaster Tycoon series, SimCity, and other similar franchises.

Isometric

I love the isometric art style of a lot of older games. It adds a lot of depth and detail to an otherwise two-dimensional game.

Pixel Art

While most 90s/early 2000s games of this style used pre-rendered grahpics, I love pixel art more than anything - especially the old GameBoy color style. I hope to implement this into my games.
